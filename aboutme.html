Author position

My name is Luiza Palsi (2000,Tallinn) and I assosiate myself as a art enthusiast. Doing art is a form of therapy for me. I mostly specialise in still life, usinng pencil or oil colors. As for my materials I use canvases or paper. It is seen in my works, that they are dull and dark shades in use, with contrasting tones and monotones. I have no particular style, since multiple of my works have been done as a lessons exercise in art schools. That is why I think I am a perfect candidate to study in graphic design or product design field. Meaning that I will be working with partners on group projects. I see myself visualise peoples ideas in to reality with my capabilities. My biggest inspiration are music videos, older artist and the vibe I am getting from them. As for easier self reflection I enjoy scrolling though Instagram and get inspired from pictures color stories. I have finished Viimsi Artschool in 2017 and went to Tallinn Artschool for two years. Even for my highschool thesis I had art related work, naming “Three artworks in different techniques”. I am finishing webdesign and liberal arts courses in Estonia Art Academy. As for my hobbies, I am a make up artist. For the future plans, I want to find my art style and learn photography,sculpture and installation.
